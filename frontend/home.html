About Us

Welcome to Indian Eatery, your authentic taste of India in Cardiff! We are passionate about bringing you the rich and diverse flavors of Indian cuisine, prepared with the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes. Our menu features a wide array of dishes, from classic street food like Samosas and Chole Bhature to flavorful curries, dosas, and refreshing beverages like Mango Lassi. Whether you're craving a quick snack or a hearty meal, we strive to provide a delightful culinary experience that transports you to the heart of India.
